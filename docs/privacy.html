Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Digicenna ("we", "our", "us") provides a telemedicine and health monitoring mobile application (the "App") that connects patients and healthcare providers. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account InformationName, email address, phone number, and password when you register as a patient or provider.
  • Health InformationVital signs, measurements (such as ECG data), medical history, appointment details, and reports you or your provider add to the App.
  • CommunicationsMessages, video/audio consultation data, and notifications exchanged through the App.
  • Device PermissionsCamera and microphone access (for video consultations), photo library access (for uploading documents/reports), and push notification tokens.
  • Usage DataApp interactions, device type, and log information used to maintain and improve the App.

How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver CareTo facilitate appointments, video consultations, and secure messaging between patients and providers.
  • Health TrackingTo record and display your vital signs and health reports within the App.
  • NotificationsTo send appointment reminders and important updates.
  • Support & ImprovementTo respond to support requests and improve App reliability and features.

How We Protect Your Information

We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted network communication, to protect your data. Health and account data is only accessible to authorized users (you and, where applicable, your treating provider).

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal or health information. Information is shared only: (1) between a patient and their healthcare provider as part of the consultation, (2) with service providers who help us operate the App (such as cloud hosting and notification delivery) under confidentiality obligations, or (3) where required by law.

Your Choices

  • Access & CorrectionYou can review and update your profile information within the App.
  • Account DeletionYou may request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us.
  • PermissionsYou can enable or disable camera, microphone, photo, and notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at support@digicenna.com.
