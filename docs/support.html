How can we help?

If you're having trouble with the Digicenna app — login issues, appointment booking, video calls, notifications, or anything else — reach out and we'll help you get sorted.

Contact us

Email us at support@digicenna.com and we'll respond as soon as possible.

Frequently asked questions

  • I can't log in to my accountMake sure you're using the email and password registered with Digicenna. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen, or email us for help.
  • My video consultation won't connectCheck your internet connection and make sure the app has camera and microphone permissions enabled in your device settings.
  • I'm not receiving notificationsConfirm notifications are enabled for Digicenna in your device settings, and that you're connected to the internet.
  • How do I delete my account or data?Email us at support@digicenna.com with your account details and we'll process your request.
